## Sending tips

- **One recipient at a time**, addressed by name and office. No mass BCC.
- **Right desk:** state IT / e-Governance secretary, District Collector, Municipal
  Commissioner, or a department's nodal officer. For NITI Aayog, the relevant
  vertical.
- **PDF attachments** (one-pager + proposal), **links** for the demo and video.
- Keep the body under ~200 words; let the attachments and demo carry the detail.
- Never claim an authorization you don't have. "Proposal / pilot" everywhere.
